ℹ️ How these interact

  • Pension access age — the earliest you can draw from your pension pot. The 25% lump sum is also gated on this age.
  • State pension age — when the "Other Annual Income in Retirement" figure starts being paid. This is treated as separate from your personal savings.
  • If your desired retirement age is earlier than the pension access age, the ISA bridges the gap. Any shortfall is flagged.
